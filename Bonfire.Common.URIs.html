        Examples 

        iex> canonical_url(%{canonical_uri: "http://example.com"})
"http://example.com"

iex> canonical_url(%{peered: %{canonical_uri: "http://example.com"}})

iex> canonical_url(%{character: %{canonical_uri: "http://example.com"}})
